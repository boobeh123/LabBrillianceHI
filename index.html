Our Story

LabBrilliance HI was created with a simple idea: beautiful diamonds should be accessible without unnecessary markups. We specialize in IGI-certified lab-grown diamonds, carefully sourced to prioritize exceptional cut, bright color, and eye-clean clarity. Every stone is selected with intention to ensure it delivers the brilliance and presence people expect from a diamond.

At the heart of LabBrilliance HI is the role of a diamond curator. Rather than offering endless inventory, each stone is thoughtfully sourced and selected from trusted suppliers based on quality, beauty, and value.

Our Focus

LabBrilliance HI centers around the diamond itself. Clients can purchase loose stones or choose from classic made-to-order settings such as solitaire rings, stud earrings, and pendants.

By working directly with suppliers and maintaining a streamlined sourcing model, we are able to offer high-quality diamonds that are fair, transparent pricing. The focus is simple — timeless pieces that highlight the stone, without unnecessary complexity.

This is a more intentional personalized approach to buying diamonds.

Before You Buy

Our Policies

We appreciate your understanding and are honored to help you find the perfect diamond.

Payment is required to secure your diamond and begin production. For sourced diamonds, I will first provide options for review before anything is secured.
Payment is currently accepted via Venmo. Additional options may be available upon request.

Diamonds are not held without payment. Once you select your preferred stone, it can be secured with full payment.
Due to availability, stones may sell if not reserved.

Made-to-order pieces use pre-designed settings paired with your selected diamond.
Custom design is fully tailored to your vision, with a rendering created before production.

All pieces are made to order and considered final sale. Because each diamond and piece is selected specifically for you, returns and exchanges are not accepted.

Simple settings such as solitaire rings, earrings, and pendants typically take 3-4 weeks.
Custom designs generally take 5-6 weeks.

All orders are shipped fully insured with tracking.
Signature confirmation is required upon delivery to ensure security.
